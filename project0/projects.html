  • Snakes and Ladder

    Snakes and Ladders is an ancient Indian board game, played between two or more players on a game board having gridded squares. A number of "ladders" and "snakes" are pictured on the board, each connecting two specific board squares. The objective of the game is to navigate according to die rolls from the start to the finish, helped or hindered by ladders and snakes, respectively.
