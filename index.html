About me!

I am RNV sundeep and I am this page talks about my journey in MSIT program IIIT-Hyderabad. I started my journey in MSIT in the month of June 2019. I left my previous organization in the month of January 2019, due to non IT sector. I wanted to have a job in IT sector. I came to know about MSIT through one of my friend,who is successfully placed through MSIT, when I was looking for other options to get into IT sector. So, I started preparing for GAT(Enterence for MSIT) and appreared for exam in the month of April. I successfully cracked the exam and got a seat in MSIT.

I chose MSIT because of two main reasons.
1. M.Tech degree: I always wanted to do M.Tech in a well reputed college and get a master's degree. This will add weight to my resume and increase my chance of getting selected.
2. Placements: MSIT provides many placement offers in top MNC's like Amazon, TCS etc. with good package.

After careful consideration and much thought, I chose MSIT because I would be getting a degree with good placement with good package within 1 year. What else would I need?

Fast forward 10 months, here I am, doing my 1st year, 2nd sem.

MSIT provides a 2 year, 3 semesters (6 mini-semesters) a year, post graduate IT education which emphasizes project-based curriculum. This course is offered to Graduates from any stream.
The specialization that are provided by this organization are:

  • Machine Learning
  • Data Analytics & Data Visualization
  • Cyber Security
  • Computer Networks and Information Security
  • E-Business Technologies
  • Software Engineering
  • Blockchain Technologies
